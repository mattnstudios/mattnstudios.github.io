ABOUT

I am MattN. I am a 20 year old director, musician, and game developer.
I exist to create honest art that inspires love, strength, empathy, and constructive action in the universe.
I refuse to rely on big studios, record labels, or "the industry" for means of production.
I feel it takes the honesty out of the art.
I'm going to create on my own terms, and trust that my art reaches whoever it's meant to reach.
I hope my work resonates with you.
